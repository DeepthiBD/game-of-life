## The Vision

> **Every child in India, regardless of background, has access to a trusted life navigation partner that helps them discover who they are, where they want to go, and how to get there.**

LifePilot is a life skills platform — not a school app, not a productivity tool, not a parental control system. It is the co-pilot that helps children aged 8–18 develop the self-awareness, decision-making, resilience, and future-orientation they need to navigate life.

---

## The Problem

India has 450 million children aged 5–18. They face:

- **Identity confusion**: No structured space for self-reflection or values development
- **Future anxiety**: Pressure to "pick a career" without exploration
- **Financial illiteracy**: No early, engaging financial education
- **Limited life skills**: Academic system optimised for examinations, not life navigation
- **Digital risk**: Most digital products for children are either entertainment or assessment tools

---

## The Opportunity

- NEP 2020 mandates life skills and experiential learning — LifePilot is aligned
- India's middle-class parenting culture values character development
- 500M+ smartphone users in India — mobile-first product is the right channel
- Offline-first is essential — connectivity is unreliable for 40% of the target market
- 10 Indian languages — localisation is a competitive moat

---

## The Product

LifePilot is an **Offline-First Life Skills Platform PWA** with 8 core modules:

| Module | Life skill developed |
|--------|---------------------|
| Cockpit | Self-awareness, daily intention |
| Pilot Profile | Identity, values, story |
| Flight Plan | Goal-setting, planning, commitment |
| Flight Log | Reflection, journalling, emotional intelligence |
| Future Me | Future orientation, long-term thinking, hope |
| Career Explorer | Career awareness, curiosity, possibility |
| Money Quest | Financial literacy, decision-making, responsibility |
| Life Choices | Values, ethics, consequences, empathy |

## The Business Model (planned)

| Tier | Audience | Model |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Free** | All children | Core 8 modules, offline, all languages |
| **Premium** | Families | Advanced career roadmaps, premium content, family goals |
| **School** | Schools, NGOs | Classroom cohorts, teacher facilitator, progress reporting |
| **Enterprise** | Corporates, CSR | Employee children programmes, CSR impact reporting |

---

## Expansion Roadmap (high level)

- **Wave 1**: Core 8 modules (offline, all languages, PWA)
- **Wave 2**: Domain structure migration, repository maturity
- **Wave 3**: Parent dashboard, family goals, copilot view
- **Wave 4**: Premium content, advanced career roadmaps
- **Wave 5**: School cohorts, facilitator tools, NEP reporting
- **Wave 6**: AI Coach (XP-004 compliant), enterprise/CSR
