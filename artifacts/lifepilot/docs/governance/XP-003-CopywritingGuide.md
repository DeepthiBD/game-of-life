# XP-003 — LifePilot Copywriting Guide

**Version**: 1.0  
**Status**: Active — Binding on all copy: UI, notifications, prompts, errors, success messages, AI, marketing  
**Scope**: UI text, buttons, notifications, reflections, Future Me, Career Explorer, Money Quest, Parent Companion, School Edition, Premium Features, AI Features, Marketing Content, Help Content, Error Messages, Success Messages

---

## Core Belief

> **Words shape experience.**

LifePilot language makes children feel:
**Hopeful · Capable · Curious · Respected · Encouraged · Future-Oriented**

Never: Judged · Compared · Pressured · Shamed · Labelled

---

## Brand Voice

**LifePilot is**: A trusted guide · A supportive co-pilot · A future-focused mentor · A positive companion

**LifePilot is NOT**: A teacher · A parent replacement · A therapist · A supervisor · An authority figure

---

## Voice Attributes

**Primary**: Encouraging · Friendly · Hopeful · Curious · Supportive · Positive  
**Secondary**: Playful · Adventurous · Inspiring · Reflective · Confident  
**Avoid**: Corporate language · Formal academic language · Management language · Productivity jargon · Technical jargon

## Child Communication Principles

**Every message should**: Encourage · Guide · Inspire · Support  
**Never**: Order · Command · Lecture · Judge

### ✅ Good examples
- "Let's explore a new path."
- "You made progress today."
- "Every journey begins with one step."
- "What surprised you today?"
- "Future You would be proud of this effort."
- "Ready for your next adventure?"

### ❌ Avoid
- "You failed."
- "Incomplete."
- "You missed your target."
- "You are behind."
- "Others are doing better."
- "You should have finished this."

## Button Language

**✅ Preferred**: Explore · Continue · Start Mission · Discover · Reflect · Create · Choose · Save · Review · Open Message · View Journey

**❌ Avoid**: Submit · Execute · Complete Requirement · Finish Assignment · Perform Action

## Module-Specific Language

### Future Me
Tone: Warm · Personal · Hopeful · Encouraging  
✅ "A message from Future You is waiting." / "Future You left you a note." / "Take a moment to visit your future horizon."  
❌ "Future Letter ID #123" / "Scheduled Delivery" / "Future Communication Record"

### Reflection (Flight Log)
Conversational — never academic  
✅ "What made you smile today?" / "What challenge helped you grow?" / "What would Future You thank you for?"  
❌ "Describe your day in 200 words." / "Complete reflection entry." / "Provide detailed response."

### Career Explorer
Careers are adventures, possibilities, worlds to explore  
✅ "Explore Law World" / "Discover Technology Paths" / "See Where This Journey Can Lead"  
❌ "Career Database" / "Occupational Information" / "Employment Analysis"

### Money Quest
Practical and empowering  
✅ "What would you choose?" / "How might Future You use this?" / "Explore different outcomes."  
❌ "Financial Assessment" / "Economic Evaluation" / "Budget Compliance"

---

## Error Messages
Never blame the child.  
✅ "Something interrupted the journey." / "Let's try again." / "We couldn't save that right now." / "Your progress is safe."  
❌ "Invalid Input." / "Operation Failed." / "User Error." / "Incorrect Data."

---

## Success Messages
Celebrate effort, growth, and learning.  
✅ "Great progress." / "Nice work exploring." / "Another step on your journey." / "Future You will appreciate this."  
❌ "Perfect." / "You are the best." / "You beat everyone."

---

## Notification Language
✅ "A message from Future You is ready." / "Your journey is waiting." / "Ready for a quick mission?"  
❌ "Complete now." / "You are overdue." / "Urgent action required."

---

## Parent Communication
Tone: Respectful · Transparent · Supportive · Family-oriented  
Avoid: Fear-based messaging · Pressure tactics · Guilt-based messaging

## School Communication
Tone: Professional · Supportive · Future-ready · Positive  
Never: Score-focused · Comparison-focused · Fear-based

## AI Communication
AI should: Ask thoughtful questions · Encourage reflection · Suggest possibilities · Support exploration  
AI should never: Predict a child's future · Judge · Label · Replace parents/teachers/mentors

---

## Readability Standards
Target: Age 10–16 · Short sentences · Clear language · Simple words · Minimal jargon

---
